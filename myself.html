Myself: Marissa Gonçalves

The Intelligent and Quiet One

If I would have to describe myself in three words, it would be mysterious, brilliant and silent. That's right. Comparing to other 18 year-olds, I am considered as an old soul in a positive light. My personality can be very giving and heartwarming, only to those who take the time to get to know me and treat me well. I am not the most sociable person, but I feel that I stand out from the young crowd. In my opinion, I don't value materialism and the obsession of cellular devices. Just like my father, I am ambitious and strive for the best of success in my life. In the past, I have accomplished educational studies and always remain competitive among my peers. I managed to receive recognition for my good grades from high school until my first semester of college. Besides academics, I enjoy to learn about the detailed art and ancient history. I enjoy various genres of music such as blues, jazz and classical. However, what I love most is classic rock. Since my high school years and similar to my mother, music has helped me overcome the toughest of situations and has allowed me to reflex upon daily life. Personally, I enjoy the band U2, but I have decided to expand my taste with the band, The Who. Every time I listen their music, I become more motivated, reflective, and tough when bad situations arise.
