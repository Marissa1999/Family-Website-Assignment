My Fearless Father: Paulo Gonçalves

The Tough and Determined One

My father is a very strong and intelligent man. As a child, he was always determined to perform his best in everything he does. He would always study, exercise and do something to improve his health. Again, just like the other close members of my family, he is considered as a very special dad. He is not the typical dad, who would go to the bar and stay with his friends. He is known for being loyal with my mother and does his best to protect us from harm. As my father grew up, he was a smart individual and very sensitive while people judge him. He always needed to improve himself and do more than the crowd. As a tough man, he flourished in his studies and managed to beat other competitors when it comes to advanced physics, mathematics and physical education. He has always been strong and brave, with no room of having a vast social life. According to his current interests, he enjoys hearing the news all day, constantly educating himself through academic articles, camping and watching sports such as hockey and especially soccer. If you would meet him in person, he might come off as very defensive and not the friendliest person like my brother or mother. However with some respect, he will calm down and express himself similar to a gentleman.
