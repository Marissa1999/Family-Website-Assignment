My Fantastic Family Reunion

Where Everyone Can Be United and Having Fun as One

My most recent reunion that I can remember, dates back to the day my Italian cousin, Nadia got married with a proud Greek man named John in 2016. My aunt Rita and my uncle Rinaldo prepared various plans to organize the best wedding possible for them. My closest relatives such as my grandparents in the Gonçalves family, the entire Cannella family, my uncle's extended family, and John's large family on both sides of his family. Overall, the wedding was amazing and very fun. My cousin really made all of us involved in all activities and we danced the night away. The Sheraton reception hall served everyone ample amounts of delicious food. I was also content when my cousin chose me to be her bridesmaid. I was honored when she allowed me to help her. My other cousins and extended relatives in the Cannella family were also glad to see me as well. It has been so long that we haven't talked to one another. Since we all have very busy lives, it is very rare to come to together and celebrate as one. However, I'm so glad that this experience turned out to be very positive and it will be treasured in my heart for a long time. I hope everyone will get together again really soon, because I would like to have the same heartfelt feeling inside, like I did before.
