My Magnificent Mother: Lilly Cannella

The Sympathetic and Nurturing One

When you feel left out, alone and isolated from the world, my mother has the capabilities to heal those deep emotional wounds. As a person, my mom grew up to be a very compassionate and thoughtful person in her heart. Working as a blood drawer in St. Mary's Hospital, my mother meets people everyday with all kinds of personalities. Since she got this job, she developed her social and technical skills required to succeed, even with a large number of patients. If you do get the chance to meet her, she will be very shy at first. However with time, she will be in her comfortable zone, making many hilarious jokes. She is the type of mom who is not obsessed with the pressures of other competitive mothers. She is very simple, approachable and patient with anyone who is nice and respectful towards her. Along with her fantastic personality, she loves listening to music in her free time. Preferably, she adores the band U2. Ever since as a teenager, she decided to take a chance and listen to their music. A few albums later, she gained a deep appreciation for the band due to the positive messages and great melodies. Other catchy 80s music in general lights up her world and get through the pressures of rough society. Music made her how she is today: confident and independent.
